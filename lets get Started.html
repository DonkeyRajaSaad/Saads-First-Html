Lets Bake A Cake

Materlials Required

  • A Bowl
  • A whisker/ Mixer
  • A unbreakable glass square/circle bowl

    • Ingredients required

  • 2 (raw)eggs
  • 1/4 glass of milk
  • 1/2 glass of sugar
  • 1 glass of all purpose flour/Maida
  • 2 spoons of oil
  • 1 pich of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of Vanilla Escence

Method

  1. Break the eggs into the same bowl.
  2. Add the milk
  3. Then add some sugar
  4. Mix Well, No crystals of sugar should be left. They might burn the cake.
  5. 1/2 glass of Maida.
  6. Mix well
  7. Then add one more half glass of maida.
  8. Now add 1 pinch of baking powder
  9. Mix well
  10. Add 2 spoons of oil.
  11. Now add 1 pinch of vanilla escence.

Settings on oven

  • If its a Manual oven put the temperature on 180 degrees and bake the cake for 45 minutes.
  • If its a Digital oven, put the setting on micro and bake to 3 min 30 seconds
